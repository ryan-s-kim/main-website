About Our Store

J - City Pop Record Store is a shop dedicated to curating authentic Japanese City Pop records from the 1970s and 1980s. Our collection features timeless artists like Tatsuro Yamashita, Anri, and Mariya Takeuchi - the sounds that defined Tokyo's neon nightlife era.

Out in-person location is at 6834 Hollywood Blvd, Los Angeles, California or email: J - City Pop Record Store@someemail.com
